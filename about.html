Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that fosters intellectual growth , critical thinking , and commitment to life-long learning. Through innovative teaching, research , and service , we aim to inspire our students to achieve their full potential and to make a positive impact in their chosen fields and communities.

To be a leading institution in medical education, renowned for cultivating compassionate, innovative, and skilled healthcare professionals who are dedicated to advancing medical science and improving patient care on a global scale.
