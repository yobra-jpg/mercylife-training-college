About Mercylife Training College

Mercylife Training College is a healthcare training institution established under the management of Mercylite Hospital Limited.

The college is committed to producing competent, compassionate, and skilled healthcare professionals through quality education, practical training, and mentorship.

Students benefit from practical exposure and clinical experience within the hospital environment at Mercylite Hospital Limited.

Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that fosters intellectual growth , critical thinking , and commitment to life-long learning. We are dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive community where students are prepared to lead and contribute to a global society. Through innovative teaching, research , and service , we aim to inspire our students to achieve their full potential and to make a positive impact in their chosen fields and communities.

Vision

To be a leading institution in medical education, renowned for cultivating compassionate, innovative, and skilled healthcare professionals who are dedicated to advancing medical science and improving patient care on a global scale.
