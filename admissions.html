Registration Requirements

REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS

The following documents and items will be required during registration at the institute.

1. Two (2) Colored Current Passport Size Photographs.

2. Attach to this letter, copies of KCSE Result slips or Certificates, Leaving Certificate, Identity Card and Birth Certificate and carry the Originals for verification.

3. Attach to this letter a duly completed Medical Examination Certificate (Attached as Appendix) by a Registered Medical Practitioner.

4. Attach to this letter a duly completed and signed Student Registration form (Attached as Appendix) with the Declaration Certificate.

5. All Students are required to make and retain a copy of this Admission Letter after admission for future use.
